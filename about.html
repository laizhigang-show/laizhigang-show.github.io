About

Lai Zhigang was born in 1963 in Bengbu, Anhui Province, and now lives in Beijing and Shenzhen. 2001, he entered the Department of Chinese Painting of the Central Academy of Fine Arts for further study. 2002, he joined the master's course of "Jia Youfu Studio" of the Central Academy of Fine Arts. In 2005, he entered the "Li Xiaoke Art Studio" of the Beijing Academy of Painting for training. In 2005, he joined the "Li Xiaoke Art Studio" of the Beijing Academy of Fine Arts for training. He was awarded the "Golden Color Award" by China Fine Arts. Now he is the president of the Contemporary Ink Painting Research Association.

Lai Zhigang‘s paintings are very impressive! The large size of his work is not empty; it has a lot of connotations. Chinese ink and wash paintings of such a large size are usually difficult to stand up if they are not well grasped. Still, the connotation of his brush and ink, his ability to create such large paintings with total durability, is not simple, which shows that his grasp and understanding of the traditional language of Chinese painting is very accurate. The small size of his works is not simple either, but they have ample space and a large layout, which also gives me a visual impact. His ink painting is very heavy and ethereal, and we can see from his works that there is both inheritance and development.
